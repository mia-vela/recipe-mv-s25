Honey Garlic Chicken Stir-Fry

Ready in 20 minutes | Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon oil (olive or sesame)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, and garlic to make the sauce.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the diced chicken until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add the bell pepper, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in the sauce and stir to coat everything evenly. Cook for 1-2 more minutes.
